Common Rodent Problems in Lexington Homes

Lexington homeowners frequently encounter several rodent species, including:

  • House mice
  • Norway rats
  • Roof rats
  • Field mice

Signs of a Rodent Infestation

Early detection is crucial for effective rodent control in Lexington MA. Watch for these warning signs:

  • Droppings near food sources or in dark corners
  • Gnaw marks on walls, furniture, or electrical wiring
  • Unusual scratching sounds in walls or attics
  • Grease marks along walls and floorboards
  • Nesting materials in hidden areas

Health Risks and Property Damage

Rodents pose significant risks to Lexington residents, including:

  • Disease transmission through droppings and urine
  • Electrical fire hazards from chewed wiring
  • Structural damage to insulation and building materials
  • Contamination of food and living spaces
