Common Wildlife Threats in Lexington Homes

Lexington residents frequently encounter various wildlife species that can cause significant property damage, including:

  • Raccoons damaging roof structures and attics
  • Squirrels chewing through electrical wiring
  • Bats colonizing attic spaces
  • Snakes entering through foundation gaps
  • Birds nesting in vents and chimneys

Essential Prevention Strategies

1. Secure Entry Points

One of the most effective ways to prevent wildlife damage is to identify and seal potential entry points. Our wildlife control experts in Lexington MA recommend:

  • Inspecting roof lines and soffits regularly
  • Sealing gaps larger than 1/4 inch
  • Installing proper vent covers
  • Checking foundation for cracks

2. Maintain Your Property

Regular maintenance is crucial for wildlife prevention:

  • Trim tree branches away from your roof
  • Keep grass cut short
  • Remove fallen fruit and berries
  • Store firewood away from your house

3. Protect Your Attic

Your attic is particularly vulnerable to wildlife invasion. Our attic wildlife removal service specialists recommend:

  • Installing proper insulation
  • Maintaining good ventilation
  • Regular attic inspections
